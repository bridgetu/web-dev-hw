Here's Some General Info...

My name is Bridget and I have a passion for all things floral. The concept for this site stemmed from my love for creating original and unique works of art.

The project that is featured on this site is meant to show that flowers can continue to live on forever even after they "die". Drying flowers and using them to create crowns, arrangements, wreaths, etc. is a beautiful way to recycle a beautiful product.

I encourage you to explore and experiment with this project. The sky is the limit!
